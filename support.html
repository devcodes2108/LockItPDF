FAQ

lockHow do I encrypt PDFs?

Upload one or more PDF files, enter a strong password, and choose regular or recovery encryption.

keyHow do I recover a forgotten PDF password?

Use the Recover page. Upload the same recovery-enabled PDF, answer your security questions, and the password will be shown after the answers match.

helpWill the PDF itself show a Forgot Password button?

No. PDF readers show their own password prompt before opening the file. Recovery happens through the LockItPDF Recover page.

database_offAre files or passwords stored?

Temporary files are used for processing. Recovery-enabled PDFs contain embedded recovery data and should be kept private.

Documentation

  1. Upload PDFs and use a strong password.
  2. Choose regular encryption or recovery encryption.
  3. Download the encrypted PDF or ZIP when processing completes.
  4. Use recovery only for private files you control.

LockItPDF does not keep your encryption password in a server database. Recovery-enabled PDFs contain embedded recovery data, so keep them private.

Contact

LockItPDF does not store your files or passwords. Recovery option is for local/private use only.
